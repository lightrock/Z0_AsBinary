# Pre-Maxwell Electromagnetic Receipt Ledger

## Status

This is a working historical receipt ledger, not a complete history of
electricity and magnetism.

The purpose is to itemize what Maxwell inherited before he wrote the field
synthesis. Through the process-first / closure-machine lens, these people did
not merely discover isolated facts. They exposed machine receipts:

```text
charge can be stored
charge can be transported
current can persist
materials resist and conduct
currents produce magnetic action
changing magnetic relations induce current
field-like relations carry stress, memory, and closure
source, boundary, and potential relations constrain field behavior
```

Maxwell's genius was not that he invented every receipt. His genius was that he
saw the receipts belonged to one machine.

---

## Lens used here

For each entry, separate:

```text
historical observation: what was experimentally noticed or formalized
machine receipt: what that observation means through the closure-machine lens
Maxwell pressure: what Maxwell later had to preserve, unify, or explain
```

The aim is not to turn pre-Maxwell people into modern theorists. The aim is to
see which pieces of the machine were already visible before Maxwell compressed
them into a field synthesis.

## What Maxwell had to complete

By the time Maxwell arrived, the receipt pile already contained:

```text
static charge attraction and repulsion
charge storage and discharge
conduction and insulation
polarity / sign bookkeeping
continuous current sources
material resistance and conductance
electrochemical custody
current-produced magnetism
current-current forces
magnetic action from current geometry
induction and electromagnetic memory
opposition / energy custody in induced currents
boundary and flux accounting
source-free potential / boundary-value discipline
circuit closure rules
field-line intuition
```

Through this lens, Maxwell's task was not merely to write equations. It was to
preserve all of those receipts inside one machine-facing synthesis.

The later Heaviside/Gibbs/Hertz vector form is an extraordinary compression of
that synthesis. But compression is not completion.

The Maxwell-completion question remains:

```text
What running closure process makes all of these receipts appear together as one
electromagnetic interface?
```
